## What is FluxPrompt?

FluxPrompt is an AI operating system that sits between your teams and the AI models they use. Instead of managing multiple AI service logins and tracking usage across vendors, FluxPrompt provides a unified platform to connect 150+ AI models, build workflows visually, and run AI across your business processes.

You get organization-level control over model usage, token budgets, data handling policies, and access controls. Teams can build and deploy AI workflows without code, while you maintain governance and visibility over every workflow, API call, and token spent.
