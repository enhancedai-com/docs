## Hey, welcome aboard

You just joined FluxPrompt (also known internally as Enhanced AI). This page is your starting point. By the end of your first week, you should know who is who, what we ship, why it matters, and how to push code that actually runs in production.

We tried to keep this page warm and human — there are jokes — but the goal is that you finish your first month feeling like you've **always** been here, not like you're still figuring out the basics.

## Your buddy

Every new teammate gets a **buddy** for the first month. Your buddy is not a manager and not a trainer — they're the person you ping with the questions you'd feel silly asking in the team channel. "How do I open this PR again?" "Who owns this part of the codebase?" "Is this thing supposed to crash?"

<Note>
  **Your default buddy is:** Guilherme. He'll reach out on day one — if he doesn't, ping him directly. When the team grows, buddies will rotate so the load is shared.
</Note>

Buddies rotate so the load is shared. The system works best when you actually **use** it — there are no points for figuring everything out alone.

## When you get stuck

Three rules, in this order:

1. **Read the docs.** That's literally why this site exists. Use the search.
2. **Ask your buddy.** Personal DM, no apology preamble. "Hey, how does X work?" is enough.
3. **Ask the team channel.** If your buddy isn't around or the question is interesting to more than one person. We prefer "asked too early" over "stayed stuck silently".

The fastest way to lose a week here is to debug something alone that someone else solved last quarter.
